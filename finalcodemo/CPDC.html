    COMMONLY ASKED QUESTIONS


    “ I’ve never really had to apply for a job before so I don’t really know how to write a resume or anything. Can CMU help with that? “



    “ Sure! The CPDC offers help in determining what field you might be interested in, how to write resumes and cover letters, and can even help you with mock interviews so you’re well prepared when that time comes! “

    “ You know I am kind of nervous about internships. How does everyone find jobs at all these cool places? “



    “ If you are unsure about looking for jobs and internships, the CPDC can connect you with resources and help you learn how to network to expand those connections that can get you into the career you want. “
