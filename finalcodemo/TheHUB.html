    • COMMONLY ASKED QUESTIONS


    “ I lost my ID card, where can I go to get a new one? “



    “ You will have to go to the HUB to get a new ID card, but it will cost about $25 to get a new card. “

    “ Where can I go if I need to talk to someone about financial aid or my school account? “



    “ The HUB handles all of those kinds of things but you can also email your HUb Liaison, whose information can be found in the resources tabs of SIO. “

    “ A program/job I am trying to apply to needs a copy of my transcript/vertification of enrollmenet, how do I get one of those? "



    “ You can request an electronic transcript and a veritification of enrollment through SIO. You can go into the HUB to request an official hard copy. “
