OFFICE OF DISABILITY RESOURCES

Students with disabilities have the right to receive reasonable accommodations that will allow them to succeed on campus in and out of the classroom



LOCATION & TIME
By appointment online
Monday - Friday:
8:30AM-5PM
5136 Margaret Morrison Street

SERVICES
There is no fee associated with this service and all types of disabilities, both visible and invisible, are served.

1. Extended exam times
2. Special housing accomodations
3. Flexible due dates
4. Proctored exams
5. Note taking assistance
6. Distraction reduced testing
7. Preferred seating
8. Ability to record lectures
9. Books in alternate formats
COMMONLY ASKED QUESTIONS


“ At my old school I had accomodations for test-taking, but I doubt CMU has anything like that? “



“ We do! If you show a valid reason, the Office of Disability Resources can help make an accomodation plan for you, which can include a distraction-reduced testing environment, proctored tests, or anything else someone might need! “

“ I had an injury and it will be difficult to get to my room for a while, is there anyone I can talk to about that? “



“ Yes! The Office of Disability Resources handles both long-term and short-term disabilities, so if you think you might need some accomodation then you can set up an appointment with them on their website! “
